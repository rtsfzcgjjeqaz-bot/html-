## Visual Structure

Bright SaaS product hero with floating app tiles, a brand lockup, and a laptop product dashboard. The layout uses white space, pastel lavender shapes, soft shadows, and a central product screen as proof.

## Motion Structure

- pastelFieldWake: 0-18 frames. White/lavender background and soft shapes fade in.
- floatingAppTiles: 8-48 frames. App tiles slide and float into the upper area.
- brandTitleResolve: 38-68 frames. Brand and descriptor text resolve on the left.
- laptopScreenReveal: 48-86 frames. Product laptop slides in and scales to the right.
- dashboardDetailSettle: 74-99 frames. Dashboard rows and cards become readable.

## Reuse Value

Reusable for websiteHero and SaaS product overview scenes that need a clean brand plus product-screen reveal.

## Risks

- White/pastel surfaces can feel flat if shadows are too subtle.
- Long brand names can collide with the laptop.
- Product UI details should be simplified enough to read.

## Recommended Action

Generate local certification preview and wait for explicit approval.
