# Scene Props Contract

Shot 48 can be adapted into a configurable step-flow scene.

## Required

- Two step cards with title, subtitle, status, accent color, and row labels.
- A directional connector from the source card to the dependent card.
- A 16:9 Remotion composition with safe top and side margins.

## Optional

- Card titles and subtitles.
- Status labels.
- Accent colors.
- Row labels and row count.
- Connector color and direction.
- Camera push intensity.

Preview media and extracted reference clips are intentionally not included in this package.
