## Visual Structure

Dark navy brand end card with a centered-left Kitaabh logo lockup, a compact tagline pill, a small website label, and restrained bottom explanatory copy. The composition uses a blue radial field from the right side and a single meaningful glint near the tagline.

## Motion Structure

- brandFieldWake: 0-16 frames. The dark blue field brightens from the right and settles into the final brand background.
- logoLockupRise: 4-24 frames. Mark and wordmark ease upward with opacity and slight scale.
- taglinePillReveal: 14-36 frames. The tagline capsule clips open horizontally under the wordmark.
- signalGlintSweep: 22-44 frames. A short blue-white glint travels across the pill edge.
- websiteMicroReveal: 30-48 frames. Website label appears as a small secondary anchor.
- bottomCopyFade: 36-56 frames. Supporting copy appears without drawing attention away from the CTA.
- finalBrandHold: 56-70 frames. All elements settle for legibility.

## Reuse Value

This is a reusable finalCTA template for brand end cards where the product promise and brand URL must remain readable. It should accept brand text, tagline, URL, and bottom copy as props in later factory integration.

## Risks

- The tagline pill can become unreadable if text is too long.
- Over-bright glints may feel decorative instead of semantic.
- Bottom copy must stay optional and secondary.

## Recommended Action

Keep as a local preview-only candidate until visual review. Do not allow Factory runtime use before explicit approval and GitHub publication.
