# Example article template: how to prepare a renderable article file

This template only validates article file format, ArticleInput parsing, and the preview command. It does not represent real business claims.

## Prepare a title and summary
Start with a clear title, then add a short opening paragraph so the hook and recommendation scenes have grounded source text.

## Keep structured steps
1. Make sure the title can stand on its own.
2. Preserve steps or conclusions from the source text without adding unverified data.
3. If dates or numbers appear, ensure they come from the article itself.

## Record traceable facts
The sample date 2026-06-24 exists only to test evidence extraction. It does not represent real pricing, ratios, or rankings.
